These resources are an optional curriculum designed to support the implementation of Utah's K–6 Social Studies Standards. This is not a comprehensive, mandatory curriculum — using every lesson within a grade level is not necessary to cover all standards, and educators may choose the lessons that best meet the needs of their students and community.

LEAs have the duty to select instructional materials that best correlate to the core standards for Utah public schools and graduation requirements (UCA 53G-4-402(1)(a)). Posting of these resources by USBE staff curriculum content specialists does not imply official endorsement by the State Board. Educators are responsible for ensuring use of these materials complies with LEA policies and directives.
